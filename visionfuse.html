VisionFUSE

A detachable lens concept that extends memory capturing into virtuality

In the realm of Mixed Reality (MR), where personal identity merges and vanishes into digital realms, storytelling becomes vital. Imagine MR glasses with detachable lenses that capture both real and virtual moments. The modular lens, attached to a grip for easy shooting, blur the line between reality and virtuality, extending our senses into a world where storytelling seamlessly weaves together our experiences.

Shortlisted for the 2024 OPPO x RCA project
